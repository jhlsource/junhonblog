About Me

Let's start with “I know nothing”.

Hi. My name is Jun-Hon Lau and I am a local to the Halifax area of West Yorkshire, England, United Kingdom. Growing up in the picturesque village of Hebden Bridge, a constant intention that has remained throughout my life has been connecting people to living their most joyful manifestation of it.

My passions lie in the junction where craft and technique intercepts expression. With a unique creative mind, I dedicate my life to the service of humanity reaching its highest form of self-expression.

Current aptitudes include ballet, pattern draughting and manufacturing garments, design to "tech-pack", colour aptitude. Passions currently working towards include: mathematics, chemistry, computer science. aspiring future spaces for expansion: health, travel, cello, art (ceramics), technical consultation in information/digital technologies.
